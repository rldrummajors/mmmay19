Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2024

Show Name: Vieux Carré

Group: Mandarins

Location: DCI World Finals

Experience Level: DCI

Context: Vieux Carré immerses the audience in the mystique and vibrancy in the New Orleans French Quarter. The show portrayed the city's deeper cultural and spiritual layers, which went against traditional Mardi Gras portrayals. The expressiveness of the show complemented by the blue and purple uniforms ultimately earned the Mandarins a score of 92.150.
